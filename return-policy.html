Return Policy

Return Policy

  • We offer a 30 day return policy from the date of purchase

  • Eligible returns received within the policy period can be fully refunded if the refund meets the criteria

  • Returns received outside of the policy period can be exchanged for another product of equal value or applied as store credit

  • Original shipping charges are non refundable

  • Returns may be subject to a 15% restocking fee

  • Returns must be complete, in the condition you received them, and in the original packaging

  • Products that have been damaged, modified, or tampered with are not eligible for a refund unless directed to do so by our Technical Support Team

  • No returns will be accepted without a return authorization number

Notice

  • All returns/exchanges must have prior authorization from a 12Volt.Solutions representative.

  • No returns/exchanges will be processed without the request form.

  • Returns must be sent back with a tracking number. We are not responsible for lost shipments.

  • When inquiring about the delivery of a return/exchange, please have your tracking number present.
